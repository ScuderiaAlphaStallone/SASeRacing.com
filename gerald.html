Gerald Charles Paul

Senior Race Engineer - Chief Engineer, Technical Race Director, Assistant Administrator.

Charles is One of the Founding Members of Scuderia Alpha Stallone. He has been assisting Lead driver Tanaya since her Amateur Licence Career and has been the Race engineer for her since her early racing days. This however this is his first outing as a Senior executive member of a Race team, He manages the Audits of the Team's Racing action and Online Operations. He has previously worked with Sim Racing Centre inc. and was a part of the Virtual Racing Cars' official adjucatory Department for the launch of their first ever ACC Modification Update.
