Our Motto: "il più veloce degli stalloni"

"The fastest of the stallions" – This motto reflects our passion for speed, precision, and the competitive spirit that drives us to be the best.

Why We Chose This Motto:

Like a stallion on the racetrack, SAS Racing is built for both power and grace. We chose this motto to represent our drive to be faster, stronger, and smarter with every lap we race.

What It Represents:

  • Speed – Always pushing for the fastest lap times.
  • Power – Fierce competition and bold moves on the track.
  • Improvement – Constantly learning, adapting, and getting faster.

We live by this motto—not just in our races, but in everything we do. Every race is a chance to prove we are truly "il più veloce degli stalloni."
