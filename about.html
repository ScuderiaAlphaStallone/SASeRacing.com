About SAS Racing

Scuderia Alpha Stallone is an elite eSports Racing team based in United Arab Emirates, specializing in competitive virtual motorsports events mainly competing in Asseto Corsa Competizione and iRacing. Founded with a vision to push the boundaries of digital racing, we are driven by passion, precision, and the relentless pursuit of victory. Our team consists of dedicated professionals who thrive on innovation and teamwork.

Our Mission & Motto

Our mission is to dominate the virtual tracks while fostering a community of racing enthusiasts and future champions, Because the s alwasy Room for More. Our motto, "il più veloce degli stalloni"—"the fastest of the stallions"—symbolizes our commitment to speed, strategy, and superior performance.

Meet Our Team

Behind every successful lap is a team of extraordinary individuals. From our lead drivers to our technical analysts and engineers, each member plays a crucial role in our success. Their expertise ensures that every race is a testament to our dedication and precision.

Achievements

Our track record speaks for itself. From winning the 2024 F1 eSports Monza GP R6 to conquering the 24 Hours of Daytona, Winning 24 Hours of LeMans in LMGTE Class in 2024. SAS Racing continues to set new standards in the eSports community.

What Sets Us Apart

We pride ourselves on our innovative strategies, data-driven decision-making, and commitment to excellence. With cutting-edge telemetry analysis and advanced race planning, we stay ahead of the competition, delivering exceptional results on every track. Our dedicated team of Engineers and Interns do more then just ensuring the team's Cup Run goes beyond just the results.
